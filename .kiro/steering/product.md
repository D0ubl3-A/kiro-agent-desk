# Product Steering

## Product Principle

Make vague project ideas executable fast.

## Audience

- Hackathon builders
- Student teams
- Solo developers
- Anyone under a deadline who needs a clear plan

## Success Criteria

- The project is demoable in under three minutes.
- The value is visible without explanation.
- The `.kiro/` folder shows spec-driven thinking.
- The app stays focused on one workflow.
